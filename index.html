BF Quality Solutions view education as more like exploration than tourism. Learning is not necessarily easy and bite-sized, sometimes it can be challenging and more difficult but ultimately the trip is more rewarding. BF Quality Solutions provides a map to the territory and first-rate navigation skills, so participants emerge able to understand the terrain and ready to discover their path.

We are obsessed with our work being authentic and engaging. Our programmes are always customised; we simply don’t provide ‘off the shelf’ training. Our clients realise tangible benefits because what they learn from BF Quality Solutions is aligned to their own business strategies and resources. We provide real world solutions, working on real problems, not lectures or classroom theory. Our sessions always include plenty of lively practical work.

We show that professional development can produce immediate changes in behavior, shifts in performance and rapid results. Our clients usually need to hit particular business targets in a specific time-frame. We delight in the challenge to increase win-rates and sales volumes, gain new business, increase client satisfaction and increase margins.

BF Quality Solutions is legally registered as BF Quality Solutions (Pty) Ltd to provide a wide range of business and training services. We use the services of some of the industry’s top management, engineers, educational talent, along with both national and international quality assurance and control intellect and skills.

We’re a proudly 100% Black owned service provider.

EXPERIENCE

The co-founder members have vast experience in education, training and development, that is, nine years of experience. During this period they have developed procedures and policies for a number of organisations while working for Phakamani Technology College. They also provided Learnership training (NQF 1 – 4) for different companies while working for Phakamani College.

BF Quality Solutions is accredited by CHIETA and ETDP SETA as a provider. All our trainers are trained as facilitators and assessors. The executive Committee members are trained as moderators. Our Managing Directors (Mr VSF Chiloane & Ms B. Magwaza) are qualified constituent moderators.

We are confident that our experience and specialisation and other skills development projects speak volumes.

Our range of services helps global organisations succeed from a quality, cost and timeline perspective.

We bring together some of industry’s most experienced and talented staff who consistently and efficiently move compounds and/ or devices through the research, analysis, design development and implementing and evaluation stages to our market. We are committed to quality, efficiency, confidentiality and competiveness to foster long term partnership with clients – we strive to complete projects within defined timelines, with high quality and within budget.

We aim to achieve and maintain the required level of quality and commitment to sponsor and regulatory agencies.
